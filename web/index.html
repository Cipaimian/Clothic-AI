The Pipeline

How the verdict is decided

The neural models detect only observable visual attributes. Transparent, auditable rule engine renders every compliance decision.

  1. 01ImageFrame
  2. 02Person DetectionYOLO11
  3. 03Garment ClassificationYOLO11
  4. 04Attribute RecognitionFashionSigLIP
  5. 05Exposure MeasurementMeta Sapiens
  6. 06Policy RulesRule engine
  7. 07Four-ScoreScoring
  8. 08Explainable DecisionExplainer
01

Perceive

Each individual is detected and tracked, their garments classified by slot (upper, lower, footwear), and attributes such as sleeve length and hemline recognised.

02

Measure

Human body parsing computes skin-exposure ratios for each region (shoulder, midriff, thigh, knee) geometrically, never inferred from appearance.

03

Reason

A transparent, JSON-configurable rule engine evaluates the active campus profile and triggers only the rules whose conditions are satisfied.

04

Explain

Every verdict is issued with four calibrated scores with detailed explanation, the relevant handbook citation, and a verified remediation when a violation is detected.
